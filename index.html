Frequently Asked Questions

Get immediate answers about operational guidelines, insurance partners, and consulting admissions.

What are the timings for outpatient consultation (OPD)?

Outpatient services under Dr. Vinod Kumar Kandala are active daily. General hours are from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Please register online or call back ahead to schedule.

Is the Emergency and ICU section open 24 hours?

Yes, Vinod Multi Speciality Hospital provides 24×7 active emergency and critical ICU admissions. Our intensive care monitors, ambulance response, and trauma surgeons are ready 7 days a week.

Do you support medical insurance networks?

Yes, we support major corporate health insurances, third-party administrators (TPAs), and government-approved medical insurance schemes. Our front desk provides assistance with the paperwork.

Where is the hospital located in Sangareddy?

We are situated at Haritha Nagar, on the Sangareddy Bypass Road, directly opposite to the Mahila Pranganam. There is parking access on-site.
